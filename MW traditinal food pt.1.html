recipe for a classic hamburger

Ingredients

1 1/2 pounds 80% lean 20% fat ground beef or ground chuck

1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce

1 1/2 teaspoons seasoning salt

1 teaspoon garlic powder

1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

Mix with your hands until well combined.

Divide the beef into sixths.

Use your CLEAN hands to form 6 hamburger patties half inch thick.

Use your thumbs to make an indention in the middle of the patties.

Grill for about 4 minutes on each side or until your preferred look.

Instructions

1.Preheat the grill

2.In a large bowl, add the beef.

Sprinkle evenly with the Worcestershire sauce, seasoning salt, garlic powder, and pepper.

Use your hands to mix the ingredients until they are just combined.

3.Divide the meat mixture into sixths.

Take 1/4 of the meat mixture and use your hands to press it

into the shape of a hamburger patty that is about half an inch thick.

Make an indention in the middle of the patty to prevent bulging in the center of the hamburger as it cooks.
